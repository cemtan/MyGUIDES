system-information-get

Get system information

Syntax

system-information-get

Description

System information consists of a name, contact and location. The name is the same value as the cluster's name, which can also be seen in the output of 'cluster-show'. Other purposes of the fields are to provide information reported in the SNMP MIB-II System Group objects "sysName", "sysContact" and "sysLocation", and to identify a system in a notification email.

"name" may be up to 13 alpha-numeric or hyphen ASCII characters long.

"contact" may be up to 180 ASCII characters long, and be used to detail e.g. the name, phone number, address of a contact.

"location" may be up to 180 ASCII characters long, and be used to detail the address and other location details.

Examples

system-information-get


      Name: HNAS1
  Location: 123 Any Street Birmingham UK
   Contact: Fred Smith +44 121 496 0123 fred@example.com

Applies To

Cluster wide
