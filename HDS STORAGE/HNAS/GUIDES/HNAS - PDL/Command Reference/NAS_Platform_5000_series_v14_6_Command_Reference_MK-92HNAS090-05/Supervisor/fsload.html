fsload

Report FS load history

Syntax

fsload

Description

Shows the FSA and FSB load (and the overall load), for 20 samples taken at 10s intervals.

The samples are taken automatically, so this information is always available, but the most recent sample (which is at the bottom of the output) may be up to 9s old.

Applies To

Cluster node

See Also

perf

Privilege Level

Supervisor
