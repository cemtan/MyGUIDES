oplog-ctrl

Controls VLSI logging of last few inquiries & responses.

Syntax

oplog-ctrl [--reset] | [--disable | --enable | --compress-cp | --dont-compress-cp]

Description

Enables or disables the VLSI "operation log", or "oplog".

You can also use this command to control whether or not checkpoints' details are compressed (see the oplog man page if you don't know what that means).

Examples

To see whether the oplog is currently enabled:

oplog-ctrl
